# User Manual: LoanManager

## 1. Getting Started
Welcome to LoanManager. This system is designed to streamline your loan management process, from customer registration to final payment collection.

### 1.1. Login
Access the system using your registered email and password.
- If you forget your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen.
- After logging in, you will be redirected to the Dashboard.

### 1.2. Dashboard Overview
The Dashboard provides a real-time summary of your portfolio:
- **Total Portfolio:** Total capital currently lent out.
- **Active Loans:** Number of ongoing loans.
- **Overdue Amount:** Total amount currently in arrears.
- **Monthly Summary:** Performance metrics for the current month.

## 2. Customer Management
Before creating a loan, you must register a customer.

### 2.1. Registering a Customer
1. Navigate to the **Customers** module.
2. Click **+ New Customer**.
3. Fill in the details: Name, Identification Number (DNI/ID), Phone, and Address.
4. Click **Save**.

### 2.2. Customer Profiles
Click on any customer in the list to view their history, active loans, and total debt.

## 3. Loan Management

### 3.1. Creating a Loan
1. From a customer's profile or the **Loans** module, select **Create Loan**.
2. Set the terms:
    - **Capital:** The amount to be lent.
    - **Interest Rate:** Monthly percentage rate.
    - **Term:** Duration of the loan (in months).
3. The system will automatically calculate the monthly interest and the expected maturity date.
4. Review and click **Confirm** to activate the loan.

### 3.2. Loan Statuses
- **Active:** Loan is ongoing with a balance.
- **Paid:** Loan has been fully settled.
- **Overdue:** Payments are past their due date (marked automatically by the system).

## 4. Recording Payments (Abonos)
This is the core of the collection process.

### 4.1. Processing a Payment
1. Locate the customer's active loan.
2. Click **Register Payment**.
3. Enter the amount received.
4. **How it works:** The system automatically distributes the payment:
    - First, it pays off any pending interest.
    - Second, it applies the remainder to the principal (Capital), reducing the total debt.
5. Confirm the transaction. A digital receipt will be generated.

## 5. Routes and Collections
If you have collectors in the field, use the **Routes** module.
- **Zones:** Define geographical areas.
- **Routes:** Group customers into a specific collection path.
- **Assignments:** Assign users (collectors) to specific routes for the day.

## 6. Reports and Analytics
Use the **Reports** module to monitor business health:
- **Portfolio Health:** Visualize the distribution of active, paid, and overdue loans.
- **Profitability:** See how much interest you are earning.
- **Cash Flow:** Forecast upcoming collections.

## 7. Administration
Administrators can manage the team:
- **Users:** Create and manage staff accounts.
- **Roles:** Assign permissions (e.g., Operator, Manager, Admin) to control access to sensitive data and actions.
